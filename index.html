Justin   


Hsin-Ling (Justin) Hsu (Chinese: 許新翎, born October 19, 2004), is an AI student researcher and R&D engineer specializing in Information Retrieval, AI for Healthcare, Large Language Models, and Natural Language Processing. He is currently a research assistant at the Academia Sinica's Reinforcement Learning Lab, an AI team research assistant at the Far Eastern Memorial Hospital, and an AI intern at GoFreight. He is pursuing a bachelor's degree in Management Information Systems at National Chengchi University. Previously, he was a part-time software engineer at the ChainSea Information Group.

Justin has participated in various project developments, including the Taiwan Power AI Chatbot Optimization Project (optimizing training data for the chatbot "Dian Bao") and NCCUPass APP (led the AI team in product development, with over 2,000 NCCU students registered and achieved outstanding results in multiple startup competitions). Additionally, he has published several academic papers as the first author, including "MedPlan: A Two-Stage RAG-Based System for Personalized Medical Plan Generation", "DAT: Dynamic Alpha Tuning for Hybrid Retrieval in Retrieval-Augmented Generation", and "KAP: MLLM-assisted OCR Text Enhancement for Hybrid Retrieval in Chinese Non-Narrative Documents".

In December 2024, Justin's team won second place nationwide [2/233 teams; ~0.8%] in the Hotai MaaS Hackathon with a solution integrating social platforms and intelligent travel health checks. Later that month, his team secured third place nationwide [3/165 teams; ~1.8%] in the LINE FRESH Campus Competition with an intelligent dementia care platform.
